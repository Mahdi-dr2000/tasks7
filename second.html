Petra

Petra, or Petra, or Petra, is a cartographic shareholding city located in the Ma'an Governorate in the south of the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan. It is famous for its rock-bottomed architecture and ancient water canals. It was called “Sila” in the past, and it was also called “the Pink City” after the colors of its twisted rocks.
